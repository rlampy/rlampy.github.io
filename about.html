About CMSCI 256

This course is required for the Computer Science major at the Mount and is optional for the minor.

The Location

Mount St. Mary's University is located in Emmitsburg, MD. The Mount is a private, liberal arts, Catholic university.

The Department

The Mathematics and Computer Science department is located in the Coad Science Building. This department is part of the School of Natural Sciences and Mathematics.

The Instructor

Dr. Ruth Lamprecht earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science from the College of William & Mary. Her research interests include CS education, programming, simulation, and modeling.

Textbook

This course uses the book "Web Development: Full Stack" by F. Max Coller (ISBN 9780357673973).

Course Outcomes

After completing the course students will be able to understand the full stack development process, develop web applications using both client-side and server-side technologies, and apply good software development practices and coding styles.

Learning Methods

Besides the final project, lectures, labs, tests, and quizzes are among the main teaching tools used. Students must be prepared to discuss and apply relevant skills from the assigned chapters during class.
