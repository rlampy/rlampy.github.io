About CIS 265

This course is required for all CIS, Cyber Security, and Mass Communication majors at VUU.

The Location

Virginia Union University is located in Richmond, VA. VUU was founded in 1865 to provide education and advancement to newly emancipated freedmen.

The Department

The Computer Information Systems / Computer Science department is located in Ellison Hall. This department is part of the School of Arts and Sciences.

The Instructor

Dr. Ruth Lamprecht earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science from the College of William & Mary. Her research interests include programming, simulation, and modeling.

Textbook

This course uses the book "Web Development & Design Foundations with HTML5" by Felke-Morris (ISBN 9780134801148).

Course Outcomes

After completing the course students will be able to understand the web development process, demonstrate the creation of personal web site, and define basic e-commerce guidelines.

Learning Methods

Lectures, labs, tests, and quizzes are among the main teaching tools used in this class. Students must be prepared to discuss the assigned chapters during class.
